About the Collaboration

Navgurukul, in collaboration with Eternal University, offers a unique three-year program designed to provide high-quality education and skill development to students from underprivileged backgrounds. This program aims to bridge the gap between traditional education and the skills required in the modern job market, particularly in the tech industry. Overview of Navgurukul Navgurukul is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing free, high-quality education to students from underserved communities. Their primary focus is on empowering youth through education in software engineering and other technical skills, ensuring that they have the opportunity to secure well-paying jobs and improve their socio-economic status.

    Overview of Eternal University Eternal University is a private university located in Baru Sahib, Himachal Pradesh, India. The university offers a range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines. Eternal University is committed to providing a holistic education that emphasizes academic excellence, moral values, and social responsibility.
      Collaboration Details The collaboration between Navgurukul and Eternal University aims to combine Navgurukul's expertise in skill development and software engineering with Eternal University's academic infrastructure and resources. The three-year program is structured to provide students with a comprehensive education that includes both theoretical knowledge and practical skills.
        Program Structure
          Year 1: Foundation and Skill Development
            Subjects Covered: Basics of computer science, programming languages (e.g., Python, JavaScript), soft skills, and communication. Activities: Hands-on coding projects, group discussions, workshops, and seminars.
              Year 2: Intermediate Skill Enhancement and Specialization
                Subjects Covered: Data structures, algorithms, web development, databases, and advanced programming concepts.
                  Specializations: Students can choose to specialize in areas like web development, data science, or software engineering.
                    Activities: Internships, real-world projects, hackathons, and industry interactions.
                      Year 3: Advanced Training and Industry Readiness
                      Subjects Covered: Advanced topics in chosen specialization, software development life cycle, project management, and entrepreneurship.
                        Activities: Capstone projects, industry mentorship, mock interviews, and job placement assistance.
                          Key Features
                            Free Education: The program is offered at no cost to students, ensuring that financial constraints do not hinder their education and career growth.
                              Holistic Development: In addition to technical skills, the program emphasizes soft skills, communication, and overall personality development.
                                Industry Connections: Students have access to a network of industry professionals, internships, and job placement opportunities.
                                  Mentorship: Continuous mentorship from experienced professionals to guide students through their academic and professional journey.
                                    Hands-on Learning: Focus on practical, project-based learning to ensure students are job-ready upon graduation.
                                      Admission Process
                                        Eligibility: Students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds who have completed their high school education.
                                          Selection: The selection process includes an application, assessment tests, and interviews to identify motivated and capable candidates.
                                            Preparation: Prior to the start of the program, selected students may undergo a preparatory course to bridge any gaps in their knowledge.
                                              Impact
                                                Employment Opportunities: Graduates of the program are equipped with the skills required to secure well-paying jobs in the tech industry.
                                                  Social Upliftment: By providing quality education to underserved communities, the program aims to uplift families and communities.
                                                    Empowerment: Students gain confidence and independence through education and skill development, enabling them to contribute positively to society.
                                                      The collaboration between Navgurukul and Eternal University represents a significant step towards creating a more equitable education system that provides opportunities for all, regardless of their socio-economic background. This program not only focuses on academic excellence but also on practical skills and real-world experience, ensuring that students are well-prepared for successful careers in the tech industry.
